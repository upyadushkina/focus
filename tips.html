What if you can't focus because there are just too many tasks?

That happens a lot. Try this:

  • Unload your brain. Write everything down. Think of it as clearing your brain's RAM.
  • Use the Eisenhower Matrix. Sort tasks by what's important and what's urgent.
  • Try a reversed to-do list. If a normal to-do list only makes you feel worse, write down what you've already done instead.
