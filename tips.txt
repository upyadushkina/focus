Welcome to Focus Mapping!

Here are some tips to help you navigate:

• Click on control nodes to activate automations
• Control nodes have special functions like "tidy up", "pretty it up", etc.
• Regular technique nodes show information when clicked
• Use filters to find specific techniques
• Drag nodes to rearrange them
• Zoom and pan to explore the map
• Click "tidy up" to organize nodes by their order tags
• Click "pretty it up" to see a prettier color scheme
• Use the tips button to toggle this panel

Enjoy exploring your focus techniques!
